About Us

Prishtina Elite is a volleyball club born out of passion, dedication, and a vision to develop the sport in the capital. Our story begins in 2018 with the opening of the first volleyball school, Iliria, which attracted extraordinary interest from young people. This early success paved the way for the expansion of the club with new schools in Dardani, Pavarsi, and Mehmet Xhevor, creating a broad base of local talents. With the growing number and quality of athletes, the club established its first competitive team, which joined the First League. Prishtina Elite competed in this league from 2018 until 2024, growing and strengthening year after year. In 2024, the team achieved one of its greatest successes to date: promotion to Superliga B. In their first season in Superliga B, Prishtina Elite finished the championship in third place, and now, in their second season, the team is in first place, being one of the main favorites to win the league and move up to Superliga A. Prishtina Elite is known for developing young talents and local players, without relying on external reinforcements. Every success achieved so far is the result of tireless work, professionalism, and trust in our youth. We are more than a club; we are a community that grows, progresses, and aims to reach the heights of Kosovan volleyball with its own strength and passion.
